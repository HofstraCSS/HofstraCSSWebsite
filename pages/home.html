Computer Science Society



Who are we?

We're not just computer science majors. We're people who love to code, who love to build things. We're constantly looking to start new projects - whether we know exactly how we're going to build it or not. We learn as we go, and help teach eachother along the way. Websites, apps, robots - we're interested in it all. This is a place for anyone who shares these interests to get started on something new, and meet some awesome people while doing something that they love.
